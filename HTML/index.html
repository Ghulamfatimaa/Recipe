My FAVORITE DISH

Pasta with bacon and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 red papers
  • 120g bacon
  • 1 can(450g) tamatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • 50g pasta per person

Method

  1. Cut the onion,red papers and bacon into small pieces.
  2. Heat some olive oil in a pan and fry the onion,red papers and bacon
  3. Add oregano,garlic,tamatoes and water and cook for 20 minutes.
  4. Cook the pasta in a big pot of boiling water.
  5. Serve the pasta with the sauce,and enjoy!
